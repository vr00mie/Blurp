About Me

Game designer turned web developer.

One can never have too many skills under their belt. That's why I have started on the journey to become a web developer. While I still have a passion for making video games, I love exploring new technologies and learning new skills. Web development just feels like my next adventure.

I started this year out by finishing up the JavaScript course on codecademy.com and after that, I decided that the HTML/CSS course seemed like a fun next step for me. I had such a great time learning how to build interactive websites, I went onward to learn jQuery and the Parse API. Now, I feel that I have a knack for it and I want to continue through this journey.

When I'm not working or self-educating, I spend quality time with my fiance in our Dallas apartment, watching Star Trek, and cuddling our two fur children, Darby and Logan.
